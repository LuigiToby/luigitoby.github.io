Luis Alejandro Rodríguez Arenas is an undergraduate Mechanical Engineering student at the Universidad de los Andes in Bogotá, Colombia, currently in his fifth semester with a minor in Computational Mathematics. He works with the university’s SpaceTech initiative in the rocketry subdivision, where he contributes to the design and prototyping of a sounding rocket using carbon fiber-reinforced polymers. His academic interests include mechanical design, additive manufacturing, embedded systems, and electromechanical control. He is especially focused on developing efficient engineering solutions through innovation and redesign, aiming to improve system performance without unnecessary complexity.
