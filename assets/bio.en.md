Luis Alejandro Rodríguez Arenas is a sixth-semester Mechanical Engineering student at Universidad de los Andes in Bogotá, Colombia, with a minor in Computational Mathematics. He is the Director of SpaceTech Rocketry, leading the development of P.A.T.A.C.O.N. 2, a L1 high-power rocket with advanced avionics and composite structures. He is also part of SpaceTech Satellite, representing Colombia at the 2026 CanSat World Championship, where he works on an autonomous autogyro descent and control system. His academic interests include mechanical design, structural and aerodynamic modeling, manufacturing processes, and the dynamics and control of aerospace systems, focusing on efficient and well-reasoned engineering solutions.
