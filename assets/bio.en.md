Luis Alejandro Rodríguez Arenas is a senior Mechanical Engineering student at Universidad de los Andes in Bogotá, Colombia, pursuing a minor in Computational Mathematics. His interests lie in the design, dynamics, simulation, control, and propulsion of aerospace systems, particularly where these fields intersect with computation. He currently serves as Director of SpaceTech Rocketry, leading the development of high-power student rockets from concept to flight. He enjoys turning engineering ideas into real systems through design, manufacturing, testing, and iteration, and is driven by curiosity, a hands-on mindset, and a desire to develop efficient, well-reasoned solutions to challenging problems.
