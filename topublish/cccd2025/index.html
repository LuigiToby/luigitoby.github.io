Context

The CCCD 2025 Rocketry Program marked the return of student rocketry at Universidad de los Andes after a seven-year hiatus. Developed only four months after the creation of SpaceTech Rocketry, the project brought together a multidisciplinary team to design, manufacture, integrate, and launch a high-power rocket for Colombia's National Collegiate Rocketry Competition (CCCD 2025).?

Objectives

The project aimed to develop practical engineering capabilities by designing and building a complete flight vehicle under realistic technical, manufacturing, and budget constraints. Beyond the competition itself, it established the foundations for future SpaceTech Rocketry projects.
