# CV Philosophy

## Purpose

This CV is designed to communicate technical competence, academic potential, leadership experience, and engineering achievements as efficiently as possible.

The document prioritizes clarity, information density, and professionalism over decoration.

It should work equally well for:

* Research opportunities
* Scholarships and grants
* Graduate school applications
* Engineering internships
* Industry positions

---

## Design Principles

### Minimalist

Every element must justify its existence.

No decorative graphics, unnecessary icons, visual effects, or stylistic distractions.

### Utilitarian

The CV exists to transmit information quickly.

Visual decisions should improve readability rather than aesthetics alone.

### Modern

The visual language is inspired by engineering documentation, technical reports, and the design philosophy associated with companies such as Apple.

Clean typography, careful spacing, and strong hierarchy are preferred over visual complexity.

### Timeless

The design should remain professional and relevant for many years without requiring redesign.

### ATS Friendly

The document must remain machine-readable and compatible with Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS).

---

## Layout

* Single column
* Letter paper format
* Black and white
* No photo
* No icons
* No progress bars
* No decorative elements

---

## Typography

Primary typeface:

* Helvetica

Typography is the primary visual tool used to establish hierarchy.

The name should be visually dominant.

Section titles should be bold, uppercase, and consistent.

---

## Structure

1. Header
2. Education
3. Experience
4. Projects
5. Leadership
6. Awards
7. Skills

Optional:

* Publications
* Certifications
* Volunteering

---

## Personal Statement

A one-line professional statement may appear below the name.

Its purpose is to provide context, not tell a story.

Examples:

* Mechanical Engineering student focused on aerospace systems, structural design, manufacturing, and control.
* Mechanical Engineering student building aerospace systems through design, manufacturing, simulation, and flight testing.

Maximum length: two lines.

---

## Overall Impression

The document should feel closer to a well-designed engineering report than a marketing brochure.

Target qualities:

* Technical
* Professional
* Precise
* Efficient
* Modern
* Credible
