Luis Alejandro Rodríguez Arenas is an undergraduate Mechanical Engineering student at the Universidad de los Andes in Bogotá, Colombia, currently in his fifth semester, with a minor in Computational Mathematics. His academic interests include mechanical design, additive manufacturing, embedded systems, and electromechanical control, with a growing focus on aerospace applications. Luis is currently working with the university’s SpaceTech initiative, specifically in the rocketry subdivision, where he is contributing to the development of a sounding rocket prototype using carbon fiber-reinforced polymers. He is particularly interested in how engineering tools and systems can support lifestyle-driven career paths, with long-term aspirations toward working in the aerospace sector or related fields.
